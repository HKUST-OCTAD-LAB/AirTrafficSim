# Development guide

AirTrafficSim is published on [GitHub](https://github.com/HKUST-OCTAD-LAB/AirTrafficSim) under xxx license. We welcome everyone intersted to contribute to this project by reporting bugs, providing suggestions, participating in development.

## Bugs and issues

For any bugs and issues found, please feel free to raise a new issue in the [Issue board](https://github.com/HKUST-OCTAD-LAB/AirTrafficSim/issues).

## Suggestions and ideas

For any features suggestion or any ideas in general, please feel free to raise a bew discussion in the [Discussion board](https://github.com/HKUST-OCTAD-LAB/AirTrafficSim/discussions).

## Code development

We welcome everyone to contribute to the development of AirTrafficSim. You can open a new item in the [Projects board](https://github.com/HKUST-OCTAD-LAB/AirTrafficSim/discussions) to let us and others know your goal. Then, please create a new branch to develop your features. Once your work is completed, please raise a pull reqquests for reviewal. Once it is done, you work and branch will be merged to the master branch where everyone will be able to use your new feature.
